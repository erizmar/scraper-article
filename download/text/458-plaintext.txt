Overview

Kafe Bromo is the signature restaurant that offers a sumptuous buffet and a la carte menu all day. Located adjacent to the swimming pool, Kafe Bromo offers views of the lush tropical garden. Sit down to a tantalizing selections of authentic and freshly-made food, selected from a range of international cuisines.
