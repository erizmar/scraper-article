Classic Club Room

Each Classic Club Room features a separate bath and standing shower, equipped with an exclusive bathroom set such as bathtub tray, bathsalts. All rooms include an individual-controlled air conditioning, executive-bar, LCD TV with excessive entertainment channels, free Wi-Fi access, personal safe, hair dryer, torch, iron board, closet, a large working desk and comfortable lounge chair for TV viewing. The Club Lounge is provided for personalized check in and check out, serving continental breakfast, evening cocktail and all day coffee/tea.
